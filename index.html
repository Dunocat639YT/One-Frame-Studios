About Us

One Frame Studios is an independent video game development studio powered by a passionate two-person team. We specialize in creating innovative experiences that blend creativity, fun, and a deep love for gaming.


Our mission is to bring unique ideas to life, crafting immersive worlds and original mechanics that push the boundaries of imagination. With a strong focus on quality and detail, we treat every project like a masterpiece, ensuring that every frame leaves a lasting impression on players.


At One Frame Studios, we believe every story deserves to be told and every idea can become an unforgettable game. Join us on this journey as we turn frames into epic adventures!
